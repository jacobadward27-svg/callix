About Callix

An agency built on one idea: your business shouldn't sleep just because you do.

Callix Agency is an AI automation studio based in Pakistan, building chatbots, support agents, and workflow systems for businesses that are tired of losing time — and leads — to manual work.

QJ

Qaim Jan Alamdar

Founder & CEO

Qaim founded Callix Agency to bring enterprise-grade AI automation within reach of small and mid-sized businesses — not just the corporations that can afford a full engineering team.

“I kept watching good businesses lose good customers to a slow reply. That's not a sales problem — it's an automation problem.”

Callix Agency started with a simple observation: most businesses don't lose customers because their product is wrong. They lose them because nobody answered in time, followed up, or kept the process moving.

Qaim Jan Alamdar built Callix Agency to close that gap — pairing conversational AI with real business workflows, so the response is instant and the follow-through is automatic. From a small studio in Pakistan, the agency now designs chatbots, support agents, and automation systems for businesses looking to run leaner without running slower.

Every project is still hands-on: Qaim and the Callix team sit down with each client to understand exactly where time and leads are being lost, before a single line of automation is built.

Our mission

To give every business — regardless of size — an AI team that answers instantly, follows up reliably, and never stops working.

What we stand for

The principles behind every build.

Speed

An AI agent is only useful if it responds faster than a human could. We design for instant, not eventual.

Precision

Automation built on a vague understanding of your business breaks quietly. We build on the specifics.

Reliability

A system that fails silently is worse than no system. We monitor, test, and support what we ship.

Partnership

We work as an extension of your team, not a vendor that disappears after go-live.
