What we do

AI that talks to your customers, and automation that runs your back office.

Two specialities, built around your actual workflow — not a one-size-fits-all bot.

Speciality 01

AI Chatbots & Customer Support Agents

A conversational AI agent trained specifically on your business — your services, pricing, policies, and tone — that greets every visitor, answers real questions, and moves the conversation toward a booking or a sale, without waiting for someone on your team to be free.

  • Deployed on your website, WhatsApp Business, Instagram, or Facebook Messenger
  • Trained on your FAQs, product catalog, pricing, and policies
  • Qualifies leads and books appointments directly into your calendar
  • Recognizes when a human is needed and hands off smoothly
  • Answers in multiple languages, around the clock
Speciality 02

AI Automation for Business Workflows

We connect the tools your business already runs on — CRM, invoicing, inventory, email, spreadsheets — into a single automated system, so one action triggers the next without anyone copying data between tabs.

  • Connects your CRM, invoicing, inventory, and communication tools
  • New lead → auto-reply → CRM entry → follow-up sequence, hands-free
  • Automated reminders, reports, and status updates
  • Custom-built around your existing process, not a rigid template
  • Frees your team from repetitive, error-prone manual tasks
